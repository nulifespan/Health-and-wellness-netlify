Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Health and Wellness collects, uses, and safeguards information gathered through this website. By continuing to use the Site, you're agreeing to the practices outlined here. If anything is unclear, our contact details are listed at the end of this page.

About This Site

Health and Wellness publishes articles on nutrition, movement, sleep, and healthy aging. We are a content platform, not a healthcare provider, and this policy addresses only the data practices of this Site itself, not any external service you may reach through a link posted here.

What Information We Collect

Information you provide. If you subscribe to updates, leave a comment, or fill out a contact form, we collect basic details like your name and email address, only as much as needed to respond or deliver what you signed up for.

Information collected automatically. Our servers and analytics tools log technical details on each visit, such as IP address, browser and device type, referring page, and which pages you viewed. This helps us understand overall usage patterns.

Cookies. Small text files stored on your device help remember preferences and measure traffic. Most browsers let you view, restrict, or delete these individually.

How Information Is Used

  • Keeping the Site running smoothly for visitors
  • Sending updates to subscribers who opted in
  • Responding to questions or messages sent through a form
  • Reviewing traffic and content performance to guide improvements
  • Spotting and resolving technical or security issues

We don't use collected data to make automated decisions with legal or similarly significant effects on visitors.

Cookies in More Detail

Some cookies are strictly functional, remembering things like a dismissed notice. Others support analytics, helping us see which pages get the most engagement. If any third-party tools are embedded on the Site, they may set their own cookies under their own separate policies.

You're free to block or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. Doing so may limit certain site features, though the core content will remain accessible.

How Information Is Shared

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited data with service providers who help operate the Site, such as hosting or email delivery platforms, strictly for the purpose of performing their service. We may also disclose information if legally required, or if necessary to protect the rights, safety, or property of the Site or its visitors.

Links to Other Sites

Articles on this Site may link to outside sources or partner sites. We aren't responsible for how those third parties handle your information, so it's worth reviewing their privacy practices separately before sharing anything with them.

How Long We Keep Information

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described here, unless a longer period is legally required. Subscriber information, for instance, is generally kept until you unsubscribe or request removal.

Security

We apply reasonable safeguards to protect the data we collect from unauthorized access or misuse. That said, no online system is completely immune to risk, and we can't guarantee absolute security for any information transmitted to or stored on the Site.

Your Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or request deletion of the personal data we hold about you. Reach out using the contact details below to make such a request, and we'll respond within a reasonable time. Every newsletter includes a one-click unsubscribe option as well.

Children's Privacy

This Site is intended for a general adult audience and isn't directed at children under 13. We don't knowingly collect information from children under that age, and we'll remove any such data promptly if we become aware of it.

International Users

If you're visiting from outside the country where our servers or providers are based, your information may be processed and stored in a different jurisdiction. Using the Site means you consent to that transfer.

Updates to This Policy

This policy may be revised periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. Updates will appear on this page with a new "last updated" date.
